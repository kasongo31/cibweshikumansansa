Our story

Private company Cibweshi-ku-Mansansa Challenge and Retreat Camp began operations at the close of 2018. The company was conceived in 2017. The company's foundations are in the tenets of agro-entrepreneurship: perseverance, originality, initiative, and conservation of natural resources. Located on the outskirts of Mkushi in Zambia's central province, the business doubles as a campsite frequented by schools, youth groups, organisational employees, churches, and individuals looking for a location to commit to an activity programme for social skill development, team building, and survival training.

The founder of the company had the idea to open up a new resource for people to learn about and improve their proficiency in eco-related fields. 
The importance of using natural resources sustainably, particularly water, forests, and soils, is also emphasised. 
The model is built around the idea that communities can only develop and maintain agro-based entrepreneurship if they are able to make responsible use of the aforementioned natural resources.
Cibweshi-ku-Mansansa is a social agro-enterprise that gives people the chance to put their skills and knowledge to use in the real world and develop them further through hands-on experience in a natural environment. 
Due to the business model's emphasis on showing rather than telling, a number of agro-project portfolios have been launched to demonstrate the success of natural resource conservation through the creation of high-quality agricultural products for sale. As a result, it is accurate to say that the company focuses heavily on agricultural pursuits and manufactures a wide variety of goods all year.

The product range includes:

  1. a) Camping - social / eco-tourism and adventure
  2. b) Agro products such as soya beans, cassava, sweet potatoes, onion, butternuts
  3. c) Aqua Cultural Products - fresh fish and smoked fish
  4. d) Livestock - broilers, free range chickens and ducks, goats and a limited number of cattle
  5. e) Bee Keeping For Honey

Our values

  1. Hard Work: We believe in the value of hard work and strive to put in the necessary effort to achieve success. We are dedicated to consistently delivering high-quality products and services.

  2. Creativity: We encourage and embrace creativity in everything we do. We believe in thinking outside the box, exploring innovative solutions, and continuously improving our offerings.

  3. Entrepreneurship: We have a strong entrepreneurial spirit and mindset. We actively seek opportunities for growth, take calculated risks, and are open to new ideas and ventures.

  4. Sustainable Use of Natural Resources: We are committed to the sustainable use of natural resources. We promote responsible practises that ensure the preservation and conservation of our environment for future generations.

  5. Agro-entrepreneurship: We believe in the potential of agro-entrepreneurship to create economic opportunities and contribute to the growth of the agricultural sector. We aim to demonstrate the link between resource conservation and entrepreneurship, showcasing the benefits of utilising natural resources in a sustainable manner.

  6. Authentic Application of Skills and Knowledge: We provide a platform for individuals to apply and enhance their skills and knowledge in a natural setting through practical experiences. We believe in hands-on learning and the power of experiential education.

  7. Customer-Centric Approach: We prioritise our customers and their satisfaction. We strive to understand their needs and preferences, delivering products and services that meet and exceed their expectations.

  8. Continuous Improvement: We are committed to continuous improvement in all aspects of our business. We actively seek feedback, listen to our customers, and use it to drive positive changes and enhancements to our offerings.

  9. Collaboration and Partnerships: We value collaboration and partnerships. We believe in fostering mutually beneficial relationships with stakeholders, including farmers, consumers, government agencies, and investors, to create a strong and interconnected agricultural ecosystem.

  10. Social Impact: We aim to make a positive social impact by empowering small-scale agro-farmers, contributing to the growth of the agricultural sector, and driving national development. We believe in the transformative power of agriculture to improve livelihoods and build sustainable communities.

Our History

In 2017, we had the idea for Cibweshi-ku-Mansansa Challenge and Retreat Camp, and by the end of 2018, we were open for business. Our company was founded on the premise that the world needs more spaces where people can come together to learn about and practise environmentally-relevant skills.

Our campsite is a popular destination for schools, youth groups, organisational employees, churches, and individuals looking for a setting in which to engage in survival skill development, social skill development, and team-building activities, and is located on the outskirts of Mkushi in the central province of Zambia.

Our company has always placed an emphasis on the connection between agro-entrepreneurship and resource conservation, as well as the value of hard work, innovation, and entrepreneurship. We believe that communities' ability to utilise natural resources in a responsible and sustainable manner is crucial to the success and longevity of agro-based entrepreneurship.

Here at Cibweshi-ku-Mansansa, we emphasise the importance of gaining real-world experience in a simulated environment. We have implemented a number of agricultural projects to demonstrate effective methods for conserving natural resources and to increase our capacity to supply a wide variety of food throughout the year.
