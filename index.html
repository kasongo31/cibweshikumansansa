Who We Are

Cibweshi-ku-Mansansa is a social and agricultural business that provides a one-of-a-kind opportunity to put acquired skills and knowledge to use in an unaltered environment. We think that getting your hands dirty is the best way to learn and develop expertise in agricultural fields. We have launched a number of agricultural initiatives to demonstrate best practises in resource conservation and generate agricultural output for sale.

We also sell a variety of agro-products like soy beans, cassava, sweet potatoes, onions, and butternuts; aquaculture products like fresh and smoked fish; and livestock like broilers, free-range chickens and ducks, goats, and a small number of cattle. In addition, we produce honey through beekeeping.

Services
