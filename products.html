Products

We are a dynamic and innovative business located in the outskirts of Mkushi, Zambia. We are dedicated to promoting sustainable practices and harnessing the potential of agro entrepreneurship. Our range of products and services includes camping experiences for schools, youth groups, and organizations, where participants can engage in survival skills development and team building activities. We also cultivate a variety of agro products such as soya beans, cassava, sweet potatoes, onion, and butternuts, ensuring they are grown sustainably. Additionally, we specialize in aqua cultural products, offering fresh and smoked fish, as well as a selection of high-quality livestock including broilers, free-range chickens and ducks, goats, and a limited number of cattle. Our commitment to environmental preservation extends to beekeeping, where we produce pure and natural honey. We are dedicated to meeting the needs of our customers and continuously expanding our product offerings. Contact us to explore the full range of products and services we provide..
